The DiagramEditor component wired with default config only — no custom toolbar, no manual event listeners, no hand-rolled controls. Everything (toolbar, tool palette, color selects, font/size selects, undo, prompts) is handled by the component.

Selection & Move

Click a node to select it. Drag to move. Hold Shift and drag for marquee selection. Hold Space and drag to pan the canvas.

Shape Editing

Use corner and edge handles to resize. Use the rotate handle on the right side to rotate. Hold Alt while rotating to snap angles.

Lines & Points

For polyline-like tools, hold Alt and click a segment to insert a point. Hold Alt and click an inner point to remove it.

Text & Shortcuts

Press Enter to edit text on a selected text-capable shape. Use Cmd/Ctrl+C, X, V, Z, Shift+Z/Y, and Delete/Backspace as expected.
